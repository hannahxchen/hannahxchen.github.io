About Me

I am currently working as a Machine Learning intern at Cybersecurity Technology Institute, Institute for Information Industry in Taiwan. I received my B.S. degree from Department of Information Management at Chang Gung University. My research interests lie in security, machine learning, and natural language processing. In particular, I am interested in adversarial machine learning, threat detection, and pricacy-preserving data analysis. I am also interested in network analysis and data mining on crowdsourcing data. In my recent research project, I work on detecting security related Tweets and information retrieval of correlating Tweet discussions on similar exploits and vulnerabilities.

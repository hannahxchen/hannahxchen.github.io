About Me

I work as a Machine Learning Intern at Cybersecurity Technology Institute, Institute for Information Industry in Taiwan. I received my B.S. degree in Information Management from Chang Gung University. My research interests lie in security, machine learning, and natural language processing. In particular, I am interested in adversarial machine learning, threat detection, and pricacy-preserving data analysis. I am also interested in network analysis and data mining on crowdsourcing data. In my recent research project, I worked on detecting security related Tweets and information retrieval of correlating similar Tweet discussions on exploits and vulnerabilities.

Research

  • Ensemble Embedding of Information Infusion Model for Threat Intelligence Correlation (2018/09 ~ )

    Integrated crowdsourcing information to gain further insight into ongoing cyber attacks by identifing and encoding vulnerability alerts, and correlated them with the associated discussions from Twitter.

  • Security-Enhanced IoT/Wearable Wireless Sensor Network using NTRU Encryption and Blockchain (2017/07 ~ 2018/06)

    Designed new lightweight cryptosystems for IoT devices in eHealth environments

  • Health Monitoring and Management System for Elderly Care Using Facial Recognition & Data Analysis (2017/03 ~ 2017/12)

    Analyzed the elders’ health and mental conditions by tracking the data of their activities and emotions, and provided meaningful graphs and charts for medical staff.

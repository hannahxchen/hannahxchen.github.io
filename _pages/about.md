I'm a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the [School of Data Science](https://datascience.virginia.edu/) at the [University of Virginia](http://www.virginia.edu), supervised by [Alex Gates](https://alexandergates.net). My current research develops methods for measuring how AI capabilities emerge and how research translates into open-source AI infrastructure.

My research focuses on the evaluation and auditing of language models: testing whether widely used metrics and benchmarks measure what they are intended to measure, and developing white-box auditing methods that surface risks standard evaluations miss. I received my PhD in Computer Science from the [University of Virginia](http://www.virginia.edu), advised by Prof. [David Evans](http://www.cs.virginia.edu/~evans) and Prof. [Yangfeng Ji](http://yangfengji.net). My dissertation introduced sensitivity auditing with steering vectors, with applications to detecting and controlling model behaviors, such as refusal, censorship, and bias.
